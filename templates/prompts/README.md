## How to Use

1. Copy the template
2. Fill in placeholders with your specific information
3. Submit to your AI assistant
4. Refine based on results
5. Save successful variations for reuse

## Customization Tips

- Add project-specific context sections
- Include your tech stack details
- Reference your coding standards
- Adapt language for your domain
- Keep successful variations
