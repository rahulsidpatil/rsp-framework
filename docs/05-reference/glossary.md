# Glossary

## A

**ADR (Architecture Decision Record)**
A document capturing an important architectural decision, including context, decision, and consequences.

**AI-Assisted Development**
Traditional development approach where AI provides suggestions and autocomplete, with developer in full control.

**AI-Native Development**
Paradigm where AI participates as a collaborative partner throughout the development lifecycle.

## C

**Context Management**
Strategies for providing relevant information to AI assistants within token limits.

**Context Window**
The amount of text (measured in tokens) an AI model can process at once.

## P

**Prompt**
Instructions given to an AI assistant to perform a task.

**Prompt Template**
Reusable structure for creating effective prompts.

## R

**RSP Framework**
Reproducible, Structured, Pragmatic framework for AI-Native software development.

**Reproducible**
Principle ensuring AI interactions can be consistently replicated and audited.

## S

**Structured**
Principle emphasizing systematic workflows and patterns for AI collaboration.

**Session Continuity**
Maintaining context across multiple AI interactions within a session.

## T

**Token**
Unit of text processed by AI models (approximately 4 characters or 0.75 words).

## V

**Validation Pattern**
Systematic approach to ensuring AI-generated code quality.
